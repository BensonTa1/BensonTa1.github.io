Hello! This is a demo of LaTeX formulas in Jekyll using MathJax.

### Inline formula

Euler's identity: $e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$

### Display formula

\[
\int_0^\infty e^{-x^2} dx = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}
\]

You can also write multiple formulas:

$$
a^2 + b^2 = c^2
$$

Enjoy writing math in your Jekyll blog! 🎉
